About My Hobbies

I have always been a creative person just not always sure how to express it. Once my kids were grown I found that I needed to do something and watching TV was not the answer. I started out with 3d printing but found there are only so many things I can print. I then started building custom automatic watches and I enjoy it but it can also be very frustrating. I then picked up airbrushing and I really enjoy this there are so many options and things that can be painted and it lets me explore a broader list of interest. I picked up leather work as a way to complete some of the airbrushing I was doing on motorcycles.
